Prosciutto e Melone

Description

Prosciutto e Melone is a classic Italian antipasto dish that combines sweet, ripe melon with savory, thinly sliced prosciutto. The dish is simple yet elegant, making it a popular choice for summer gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe cantaloupe or honeydew melon
  • 200g prosciutto (thinly sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cut the melon in half and remove the seeds. Scoop out the flesh and cut it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Arrange the melon pieces on a serving platter.
  3. Top the melon with slices of prosciutto.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of black pepper.
