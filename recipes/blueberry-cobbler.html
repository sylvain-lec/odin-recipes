Blueberry Cobbler

Description

A delightful dessert made with fresh blueberries, a buttery crust, and a hint of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine blueberries, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1 tablespoon flour. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, mix the remaining flour, sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Add melted butter, milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until combined.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  6. Spread the blueberry mixture over the batter.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown.
